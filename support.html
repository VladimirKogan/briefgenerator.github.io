BriefGenerator – Support Page

🛠️ Need Help?

If you're experiencing problems with the app or have questions, we’re here to help.

Please contact us via email: support@briefgenerator.app

📋 Common Questions

  • I didn’t receive my generated letter
    Check your internet connection and try again. Letters are not stored unless saved.
  • I can’t log in
    Ensure your email is verified. Try resetting your password.
  • I want to request a refund
    If you purchased through the App Store or Google Play, refund requests must go through those platforms directly.

🔒 Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. No letter content is stored unless you choose to save it. For details, view our Privacy Policy.
